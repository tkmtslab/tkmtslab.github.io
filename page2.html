Activities

During the G20 summit held in Osaka between 28-29 June 2019, our lab member, Khalid who is Ph.D. 3rd year student from Saudi Arabia had the honor to be the translator to HRH the Saudi crown prince Mohammad bin Salman Al-Saud during his visit to Hiroshima city. Khalid have also had the chance to translate to a number of high officials between Japan and Saudi Arabia during the sideline meetings held prior to the G20 summit, including the governor of Tokyo Mrs. Yuriko Koike, ministers, and members of the Japanese House of Representatives. In our lab you are blessed with such diversified environment.

OPENING CEREMONY OF TOKYO INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY 2020

September 2nd year of Reiwa, following the cancellation of the opening ceremony in April 2020 due to the effects of the new coronavirus infection, this opening ceremony was held for all students enrolled in April and September. In addition, it was unavoidable to reduce the scale of the event in light of the recent infection situation. The state of the ceremony was broadcast live on YouTube's official channel.

The entrance ceremony was attended by 33 people, including the president, directors / vice presidents, auditors, guests, and representatives of each school who enrolled in April and September. Including Amin Nazarahari, the member of our laboratory selected as the representative of 2020 Fall Semester.

In the excellent research environment of Tokyo Institute of Technology, Amin-san not only obtains a degree, but also finds his own ability to contribute to society by improving his knowledge in a top-class environment and realize his dream. "We will do our best to protect our planet, our generation and future generations," he said.
